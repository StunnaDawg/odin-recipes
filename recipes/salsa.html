The Big Dawg Salsa

Ingrediants

  • Fresh roma tomatoes
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Jalapeno
  • Cilantro
  • Red onion
  • Green onion
  • Garlic
  • Lime
  • Chili powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Roughly chop the vegetables

  2. Add vegetables to a food processor

  3. Chop in quick bursts until finely chopped
